Privacy Policy – Mael Bot

Last Updated: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Mael Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and stores information when used within Discord. The Bot is maintained and operated by the team behind Mael.

2. Information Collected

The Bot may collect and store the following data:

  • Discord User ID
  • Discord Username
  • Game-related data (rank, inventory, progression)
  • Messages sent directly to the Bot for command processing and logging purposes

3. Purpose of Data Collection

Collected data is used solely for:

  • Providing RPG game functionality
  • Managing ranking systems
  • Managing inventory and progression systems
  • Responding to user commands

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely in a local database and is not shared, sold, or distributed to third parties.

5. Public Data

Usernames and ranking information may be displayed publicly within Discord servers as part of the ranking system.

6. Data Sharing

The Bot does not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties, except when required by law.

7. Data Removal

Users may request deletion of their stored data by contacting the email listed below.
